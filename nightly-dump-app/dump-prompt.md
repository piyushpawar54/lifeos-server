# Nightly Dump Prompt

Work through each department. Answer whatever feels relevant tonight — skip what doesn't apply. No perfect sentences needed.

---

## Time
- Did today go the way I planned?
- Where did my time actually go?
- What didn't get done that I expected to?
- What's the one thing I must protect time for tomorrow?

---

## Mind
- What's occupying mental space right now?
- Any unresolved decisions or open loops?
- What am I anxious or uncertain about?
- Any ideas or insights I don't want to lose?

---

## Body
- How is my energy today — morning, afternoon, evening?
- Did I sleep, move, and eat in a way that served me?
- Any physical signals I should pay attention to?
- What does my body need tomorrow?

---

## Money
- Any spending today worth noting?
- Any financial decisions I'm sitting on?
- Am I on track with what I said I'd do this month?
- Any money worries I'm carrying?

---

## Relationships
- Who did I connect with today — and how did it feel?
- Is there anyone I've been meaning to reach out to?
- Any tension or unspoken thing with someone I care about?
- Who do I want to show up better for?

---

## Environment
- How did my physical space feel today?
- Is my digital setup serving me or slowing me down?
- Anything to organise, fix, or remove?
- What one environmental change would make tomorrow easier?

---

*Finish with anything else on your mind. Press Enter twice when done.*
